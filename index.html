Biography

George Gao: A Virtuoso Reimagining the Erhu

George Gao, the Gemini-award-nominated Canadian musician, began his journey with the erhu at the tender age of six. His prodigious talent took him from the halls of the Shanghai Conservatory of Music to the prestigious Royal Conservatory of Music in Toronto. Today, Gao is celebrated worldwide as a master of the erhu, an innovative composer, and a pioneering force in modern Chinese music.

Gao’s artistry transcends the traditional boundaries of the erhu. His compositions, such as the critically acclaimed Capriccio series, have become cornerstones in the contemporary erhu repertoire. Works from "Erhu Capriccio No. 1 - Nostalgia" to "Capriccio No. 6 – Shaoyin" not only showcase his technical prowess but also his deep emotional resonance and innovative spirit.

Collaborating with luminaries like 20-time Grammy winner Brian Keane, Gao has contributed to major film and television scores, including the PBS documentary series "Becoming American – The Chinese Experience." His music, a seamless blend of Eastern and Western influences, has garnered accolades across the globe.

Beyond his compositions, Gao's performances are legendary. His virtuosic interpretations of pieces like "Carmen Fantasy" and "Gypsy Air" have redefined what the erhu can achieve. He is not just a performer but a visionary, integrating elements of jazz, rock, new age, and world music into his works, thus expanding the erhu’s horizons and appeal.

Gao's influence extends beyond the concert stage. As a professor at institutions like the Shanghai Institute of Visual Art and the National Taiwan University of Arts, he has nurtured a new generation of musicians. His innovative invention, the Shaoqin erhu, has revolutionized the instrument’s capabilities, further cementing his status as a trailblazer in the world of music.

高韶青：重新定义二胡的音乐大师

加拿大Gemini奖提名的高韶青，从六岁起便开始学习二胡。他的音乐天赋使他从上海音乐学院走向多伦多皇家音乐学院的殿堂。今天，高韶青被公认为世界级的二胡演奏家、创新作曲家和现代中国音乐的先锋力量。

高韶青的艺术超越了二胡的传统界限。他创作的《随想曲》系列已成为当代二胡曲目中的基石。从《随想曲第一号－思乡》到《随想曲第六号－韶音》不仅展示了他的技巧，还体现了其深厚的情感共鸣和创新精神。

与20次格莱美奖得主布莱恩·基恩等名人合作，高韶青为包括PBS纪录片系列《成为美国人——华人经历》在内的多部重要电影和电视配乐。他的音乐将东西方元素无缝融合，赢得了全球的赞誉。

不仅在创作上，在演奏上高韶青同样令人瞩目。他对《卡门幻想曲》和《吉普赛之歌》的精湛演绎，重新定义了二胡的表现力。他不仅是演奏家，更是音乐的先行者，将爵士、摇滚、新世纪和世界音乐元素融入其作品，扩展了二胡的视野和吸引力。

高韶青的影响不仅限于音乐会舞台。作为上海视觉艺术学院和台湾艺术大学等机构的教授，他培养了新一代的音乐家。他发明的韶琴二胡，更是彻底革新了这一乐器的能力，进一步巩固了他在音乐界的开拓者地位。
